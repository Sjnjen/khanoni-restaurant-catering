About Khanoni

Khanoni is a beloved local restaurant in Thohoyandou that specializes in hearty, flavorful meals perfect for both everyday dining and special occasions. While we take pride in serving delicious traditional dishes to our daily customers, we've also built a strong reputation for catering events of all sizes.

Our team prepares every meal with the same care and attention to quality, whether you're ordering a single plate for delivery or catering for hundreds of guests. What sets us apart is our commitment to authentic flavors, generous portions, and reliable service that has made us a trusted name in the community for years.

Delivery available in Thohoyandou and surrounding areas. Outside Thohoyandou delivery is R5 extra.
